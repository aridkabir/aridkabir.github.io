How was TWICE Formed

TWICE was formed through the survival show, SIXTEEN, a collorabation by JYP Entertainment and MNet.

The show pitted 16 contestants against each other to compete to debut in the group TWICE, where 7 members were ulitmately selected.

At the very end of the show, Tzuyu was selected as well based on fan voting to debut with the group.

Contestant Hirai Momo, who was originally eliminated ultimately debuted with the group based on discussion from the internal team.

Why should I care?

As TWICE turns nearly 10 years old this year, now is a great time to get into them as a group.

They have an incredibly rich discography, spanning many different styles and genres that will surely resonate with you.

TWICE as a group ultimately embodies the power of friendship and working together to over challenges, and has really resonated with audiences around the world.
